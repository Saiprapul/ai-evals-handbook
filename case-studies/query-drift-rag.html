Case Study: The "Holiday Season" Drift

How a 92% accurate RAG system dropped to 68% overnight.

The Timeline

timeline
    title 48 Hours of Drift
    Normal Operations : 92% Accuracy : "User queries standard"
    Black Friday Starts : 85% Accuracy : "Traffic spikes 4x"
    The Incident : 68% Accuracy : "New query pattern: 'Gift Receipt?'"
    Discovery : Alerts Firing : "Team notices low adherence"
    Fix Deployed : 94% Accuracy : "Added 'returns' to knowledge base"

The Root Cause

Our retrieval system was optimized for semantic similarity on technical support. "Gift receipt" queries were semantically matched to "Receipt generation API", misleading the LLM.
